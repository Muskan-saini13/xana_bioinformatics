Xana Cloud Solution – easy access to cloud computing

Designed for seamless collaboration and efficiency, Xana’s Cloud Solution platform enables your team to effortlessly create, share, and manage cloud resources for both compute and storage. Experience the benefits of cloud computing without the burdens. Easily manage your cloud resources so you can concentrate on what matters most.

Project Nodes & Data Storage : Scale with Ease

Empower your team to take control of their computational needs with our scalable cloud computing instances, known as project nodes. These nodes can be effortlessly adjusted to match the unique requirements of any project. Complementing this, your team can also create dedicated data storage buckets, ensuring that all data is readily accessible and securely stored. With the ability to seamlessly share nodes and storage buckets, collaboration becomes second nature, driving productivity. Rest assured, your resources are fortified against unauthorized access and safeguarded by virtual private networks and advanced security protocols.

Batch Processing : Automate & Optimize

Streamline your workflows with our Batch Processing feature. Perfect for handling large datasets, automating complex tasks, and managing resource-intensive operations like Nextflow pipelines, this feature allows users to efficiently manage queues and maximize the utilization of computational resources. Say goodbye to manual interventions and hello to optimized performance.

Administration Account : Full Control at Your Fingertips

Manage your cloud environment with confidence using our dedicated Administration Account. Ideal for managers, project leaders, and team supervisors, this feature provides a comprehensive overview of cloud usage and access controls. Easily monitor instance statuses, filter by users or projects, and keep a close eye on costs. With the ability to manage user permissions, assign roles, and oversee third-party accounts, maintaining order and security across your organization has never been easier.

Getting started is simple, whether you’re a standard user or an administrator. Login through Azure AD as a normal user or use a separate account for administrative access, ensuring a tailored experience that meets your specific needs.
