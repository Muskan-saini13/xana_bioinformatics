AI Modeling with GOSTAR®

GOSTAR® is the driving force behind AI/ML innovations in drug discovery, offering an unparalleled structure-activity relationship (SAR) dataset. This rich repository is essential for enhancing predictive modeling, streamlining the research workflow, and guiding decision-making. With GOSTAR®, researchers have immediate access to a vast pool of data, facilitating a deeper understanding of compound interactions and accelerating the journey from conceptualization to identification of promising drug candidates.

Predict Adverse Events

Advance in your safety assessments with confidence by integrating the extensive toxicity and ADME profiles available in GOSTAR. GOSTAR® supports your commitment to developing safer therapeutic solutions.

Informed Decision-Making

With access to data on 77k+ biological targets and insights from 12k+ abstracts and conferences, GOSTAR® assists AI/ML companies in making informed decisions. This wealth of information supports the prioritization of experiments and the design of novel compounds, enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of the drug discovery process.

GOSTAR® APIs: Catalyzing AI in Drug Discovery

GOSTAR® APIs power AI/ML advancements in pharmaceutical research by providing an unmatched dataset of structure-activity relationships (SAR). This valuable resource plays a crucial role in improving predictive models, simplifying research processes, and influencing decision-making. The provided APIs offer researchers instant access to an extensive database, enabling a more comprehensive grasp of how compounds interact. This accelerates the process from initial concept to the discovery of potential drug candidates.

By leveraging GOSTAR® web APIs of SAR databases, AI/ML companies gain access to a vast pool of SAR data curated from various sources, enabling a broader analysis and exploration of structure-activity relationships.

Using GOSTAR® web APIs, AI/ML companies can programmatically retrieve SAR data, eliminating the need for manual data collection and saving time. Automation also allows for regularly updating the database with new data as it becomes available.

The analysis and visualization of GOSTAR® data assist the company in making data-driven decisions during the drug discovery process. They can identify promising compounds, prioritize experiments, or design new compounds based on observed structure-activity relationships.

GOSTAR® Web APIs enable seamless integration of GOSTAR® data into the company’s existing data infrastructure and analytical workflows. It also facilitates collaboration with external partners by providing a standardized interface to share and exchange SAR data.
